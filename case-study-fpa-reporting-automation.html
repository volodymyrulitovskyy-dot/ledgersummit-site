FP&A Reporting Automation Case Study

A finance team replaced manual exports with FP&A reporting that stays live-linked to accounting data and live operating statistics.

Client profile: E-commerce and distribution business, USD 27M revenue, 5-person finance team.

8 days to 1 dayLag from close to FP&A reporting pack
DailyKPI refresh from live accounting and operations data
64% lessWeekly cash flow forecast prep time

Before automation

FP&A reports depended on manual data exports and spreadsheet stitching. KPI updates were delayed and cash flow assumptions were maintained in separate files.

  • Close completed before reporting, creating avoidable lag
  • KPI tables updated manually once per week
  • Cash flow inputs tracked across disconnected workbooks

What Ledger Summit implemented

Ledger Summit built a reporting workflow that links FP&A outputs, KPI tracking, and cash flow reports directly to validated accounting and operating data sources.

  • Structured report tables tied to live accounting data
  • Live KPI layer tied to operational statistics and accounting drivers
  • Cash flow report linked to current AR, AP, payroll, and operating inputs

Implementation timeline

  • Week 1: documented current FP&A reporting process and data sources
  • Week 2: mapped source-to-report fields and validation checks
  • Week 3: built live-linked KPI and reporting tables
  • Week 4: parallel run with leadership reporting pack
  • Week 5: production cutover and weekly forecast cadence reset

Controls and data handling

  • Source-to-report reconciliation checks before publish
  • Versioned assumptions with owner and timestamp tracking
