ME Close Automation Case Study

This client moved from a manual month-end close with spreadsheet chasing to a structured close workflow with clear owners, automated reminders, and exception routing.

Client profile: Regional distribution company, USD 18M revenue, 5-person accounting team.

11 to 6 daysClose cycle duration
58% lessManual close-prep effort
84% fewerLate-task escalations

Before automation

Close management depended on manually updated trackers and email follow-up. Dependencies were unclear and bottlenecks surfaced late.

  • Task status maintained in multiple spreadsheets
  • Approvals delayed by inbox handoffs
  • High rework during final close days

What Ledger Summit implemented

Ledger Summit implemented a close orchestration workflow with dependency logic, owner routing, and automated status checks.

  • Central close task board with dependency rules
  • Automated reminder and escalation routing
  • Exception queue for unresolved items before sign-off

Implementation timeline

  • Week 1: mapped current close SOP and pain points
  • Week 2: configured workflow rules and owner matrix
  • Week 3: parallel run with before/after validation
  • Week 4: production cutover and KPI tracking

Controls and data handling

  • Role-based approvals by close stage and account group
  • Timestamped logs for all status changes and approvals
  • Escalation SLA for overdue tasks and unresolved exceptions
