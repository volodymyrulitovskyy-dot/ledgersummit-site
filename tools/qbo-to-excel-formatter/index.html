Review Checklist

What reviewers usually validate first

These are the formatting issues that most often slow down Excel-based accounting work.

Date consistency

If dates do not normalize cleanly, sorting and period logic become unreliable.

Numeric amount quality

Amounts stored as text or mixed sign conventions create avoidable spreadsheet errors.

Missing account fields

Rows without a clear account or category often break pivots and workpapers.

Missing transaction dates

Blank dates weaken cut-off review and period-based analysis.

Thin memo coverage

Very weak descriptions make later investigation harder.

Debit and credit readability

A clearer debit and credit view often makes exported data easier to work with downstream.
