Review Checklist

What reviewers usually validate first

These are the areas teams usually discuss first once the valuation calculation is visible.

Pre-money reflects negotiated value

Confirm the pre-money is the agreed term, not a back-calculated number.

Share count is fully diluted

Include all options, warrants, and convertible instruments in the existing share count.

Option pool is pre-money

Most term sheets create the pool pre-money, diluting existing holders, not the new investor.

SAFEs and notes convert first

Outstanding convertibles increase the share count before the priced round shares are issued.

Price per share matches term sheet

Verify the calculated PPS matches the Series term sheet within rounding.

Cap table is current

Ensure the share register is up to date before running the model.
