Review Checklist

What reviewers usually validate first

These are the issues that usually matter most once the raw aging report is already in hand.

Ninety plus exposure

Balances sitting in the oldest bucket should usually be reviewed before everything else.

Large overdue totals

Big overdue balances often matter more than a high count of smaller invoices.

Customer concentration

If a small group of customers drives a large share of receivables, the team should know that quickly.

Credit balances

Credits can block clear collections follow-up if they are not handled early.

Missing terms

Aging review is slower when terms are blank and the collection expectation is unclear.

Overdue ratio

A customer with most of its balance outside the current bucket often deserves early escalation.
