Month-End Reporting Shouldn't Be a Marathon

For most mid-market companies, financial statement preparation is the bottleneck of month-end close. Skilled professionals spend days on formatting, formulas, and copy-paste - work that adds zero analytical value.

The data exists in the ERP. The chart of accounts is defined. The statement formats are known. Yet every month, the same manual process repeats: export, format, calculate, narrate, review. And every month, the same errors creep in.

EPM tools like OneStream and Planful serve large enterprises. But manufacturers and mid-market companies running SAP, Oracle, or NetSuite still depend on Excel workflows that haven't fundamentally changed in 20 years.

Meet the Client

A mid-sized manufacturer with $85M in revenue, running SAP as their ERP. Three plant locations, two legal entities, and a 5-person finance team responsible for all internal and external reporting.

Every month, the controller and a senior accountant spent 15-20 hours preparing financial statements. They exported raw GL data from SAP, copied it into a master Excel workbook, mapped accounts to statement line items, built variance calculations, and drafted management commentary paragraph by paragraph.

The process was fragile. One formula break could cascade through the entire workbook. Version control was a constant headache - "Final_v3_REVISED_Melissa.xlsx" was a running joke that nobody found funny.

"Our CFO would ask for a quick P&L update after a board question, and it would take two hours just to regenerate the numbers. We were a formatting factory, not a finance team." - Controller, Client Company

The irony: the team had excellent data in SAP. Every transaction, every journal entry, every cost center allocation was there. The problem was extraction and presentation - the gap between raw GL data and polished, audit-ready financial statements.

How We Built the AI Solution

We designed a one-click financial reporting engine fully integrated with SAP. The system connects directly to SAP's GL module and generates complete, formatted financial statements - with AI-powered narratives - in minutes.

One click. Full P&L, balance sheet, cash flow statement, variance analysis, and management narrative. What used to take 20 hours now takes under 2 - and the quality is higher and more consistent because it's generated from live data, not manually copied cells.
