Most finance and accounting leaders we talk to are stuck on the same four problems. Here's how we resolve each.

01

"Why is close still taking 12 days when our books are clean?"

Manual reconciliations, scattered approval threads, and last-minute accruals — not bad data. We compress close to 3–5 days by automating the recurring 80% and keeping humans on the judgment calls.

02

"How do we get audit-ready evidence without a six-figure ERP migration?"

You don't need NetSuite to be audit-ready. We layer audit trails, role-based approvals, and parallel reconciliations onto your existing QuickBooks or Xero — so every workflow ships with the evidence built in.

03

"Who owns FP&A reporting between me, my bookkeeper, and our part-time CFO?"

Right now, nobody — which is why the board pack arrives late and inconsistent. Our Fractional AI CFO engagement owns the deliverable end-to-end and ships a KPI dashboard your part-time CFO can run on auto-pilot.

04

"Why does every workflow improvement need 6 months of consulting first?"

It doesn't. We scope and ship one workflow in 3 weeks on your existing stack, then move to the next. No multi-month roadmap, no platform change. The first conversation is a free review — no commitment.
