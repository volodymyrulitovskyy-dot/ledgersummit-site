The Problem

Off-the-Shelf Tools Don't Fit Your Workflow

Enterprise finance software is built for the average company, not yours. You end up paying for features you don't need, missing the ones you do, and stitching together workarounds in Excel. The cost of per-seat licensing for platforms like BlackLine, FloQast, or Planful can exceed $100K annually - and you still need a team to administer them.

Custom AI tools, on the other hand, are built to solve your specific problems. No unnecessary modules. No per-seat pricing. No vendor lock-in. Just tools that do exactly what you need, integrated directly with your ERP and data sources.

We build, deploy, and maintain these tools so your team can focus on analysis and decision-making - not data wrangling.
