FP&A modules

Driver-based modeling, not static workbooks

Five FP&A modules that compose into a coherent operating cadence — board, lender, sponsor, executive team.

13-week cash flow

Driver-based rolling forecast with customer-level AR collection patterns, vendor-level AP timing, payroll/tax obligation timing, capex pipeline, and lender availability — refreshed daily.

Annual budget + rolling forecast

Driver-based revenue model, headcount + comp planning, capex pipeline, OpEx categories. Rolling forecast keeps the budget live throughout the year, not stale by Q2.

Scenario modeling

Five preset scenarios always live: base, downside (top-customer delay), upside (collection acceleration), M&A close, refinance. Sensitivity to any driver is a slider, not a workbook clone.

Variance + flux analysis

Automated variance flagging with controller-grade commentary. Every material variance traces back to the driver that moved.

Board / lender / sponsor pack

One source of truth across stakeholder packs — the same numbers leadership defends in any room. Auto-generated from the live ledger and forecast.
