Why a Fractional AI CFO

You Need a CFO Who Understands AI - Not Just Spreadsheets

The traditional fractional CFO model gives you someone who can build budgets, prepare board decks, and manage cash flow. That's table stakes. What most growing companies actually need is someone who can re-architect their finance function - eliminate manual bottlenecks, build automated forecasting systems, and create real-time visibility into financial performance.

Our Fractional AI CFO does both: strategic finance leadership AND hands-on AI implementation. We don't bring in consultants who write reports about what you should do. We build the tools and systems ourselves.

The outcome: a finance function that runs on real-time data, produces forecasts automatically, and frees your team to focus on analysis and strategy - not data entry.
