The Challenge

Why Generic Accounting Software Falls Short

Most accounting teams cobble together processes from their ERP, Excel, email, and shared drives. The ERP handles the transactions, but everything in between - the matching logic, the exception handling, the status tracking, the reporting - lives in spreadsheets maintained by one or two people who know "how things work."

Enterprise tools like BlackLine promise to solve this, but at $50K-$200K per year with 6-12 month implementations. And they still require your team to learn a new platform and adapt your processes to fit their workflow.

Our approach: we build lightweight, custom tools that fit your existing processes, connect to your ERP natively, and deploy in weeks - not months. You own the code. Zero per-seat fees.
