The Problem

Why Finance Teams Are Drowning in Manual Work

Most finance teams spend 60-80% of their time on repetitive data tasks: pulling reports, reconciling spreadsheets, fixing formula errors, and re-formatting board decks. This leaves almost no time for the strategic analysis that actually drives business decisions.

The result? Forecasts are stale by the time they reach the CFO. Variance analysis happens weeks after month-end. And scenario planning - the kind that helps you navigate uncertainty - happens once a year if at all.

We've seen this pattern across dozens of mid-market companies. The bottleneck isn't your team's talent - it's the manual plumbing between your ERP, spreadsheets, and reporting tools.
