Accruals Automation Case Study

A services business replaced manual accrual spreadsheets with standardized automation for recurring entries, support checks, and approval routing.

Client profile: Multi-location services business, USD 12M revenue, 4-person accounting team.

63% fasterAccrual prep cycle
71% fewerPost-close accrual corrections
100%Entries with linked support

Before automation

Accruals were prepared manually with inconsistent templates and missing support documentation. Corrections were common after close.

  • Manual roll-forward files by account owner
  • Inconsistent naming and support attachment process
  • Frequent reclassifications after review

What Ledger Summit implemented

Ledger Summit built template-driven accrual workflows with required fields, support checks, and approval routing before posting.

  • Recurring accrual templates by account type
  • Validation checks for amount logic and support completeness
  • Approval queue with reviewer assignment and SLA

Implementation timeline

  • Week 1: baseline SOP capture and account grouping
  • Week 2: template and validation rule build
  • Week 3: pilot with top 15 accrual accounts
  • Week 4: full rollout and exception monitoring
