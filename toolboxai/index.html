What is Tool Box AI? +

Tool Box AI is the instant enterprise upgrade to your existing QuickBooks or Xero. It layers advanced ERP capabilities — multi-entity reporting, workflow and approvals, role-based access, and audit-ready controls — directly onto your current ledger, without expensive migration or a six-figure price tag.

How is this different from NetSuite, Costpoint, or Unanet? +

Same operating-model outcomes — multi-entity consolidation, project accounting, approval workflows, executive reporting, and audit-ready controls — without six-figure enterprise ERP cost, without a multi-quarter implementation, and without ripping out QuickBooks or Xero.

Do I have to replace my existing accounting system? +

No. Tool Box AI is designed to sit on top of QuickBooks or Xero and add the ERP layer — workflow, consolidations, project accounting, and reporting — while keeping your existing ledger as the system of record.

Is Tool Box AI secure enough for enterprise deployment? +

Yes. The platform is engineered around multi-tenant isolation, role-based access, audit trails, workflow accountability, and operational rigor — and is being developed toward a strong SOC 2-aligned operating model.

Is Tool Box AI SOC 2 certified? +

Tool Box AI is being built toward a SOC 2-aligned operating model. We do not claim a completed SOC 2 certification — we are building toward the security, access, and control posture that supports one.

What does Tool Box AI cost compared to NetSuite, Costpoint, or Unanet? +

Enterprise ERP platforms typically run in the tens of thousands to low six figures per year, plus multi-quarter implementation costs. Tool Box AI is positioned at SMB economics on top of your existing QuickBooks or Xero subscription, with engagement-based onboarding rather than a full migration.

Does Tool Box AI support GovCon and project-driven accounting? +

Yes. The platform is designed to support multi-entity structures, project-driven accounting, labor distribution, indirect rate structures, and GovCon-oriented documentation flows — without six-figure enterprise ERP cost.

How long does it take to go live? +

Most deployments go live in weeks, not quarters, because Tool Box AI layers onto your existing QuickBooks or Xero ledger instead of replacing it. Timeline scales with entity count, workflow complexity, and reporting scope.
