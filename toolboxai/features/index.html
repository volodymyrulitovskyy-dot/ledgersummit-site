Core Accounting

General Ledger & Chart of Accounts

The accounting foundation that extends your QBO or Xero ledger with enterprise-grade GL capabilities, multi-entity support, and automated period management.

General Ledger

Full journal entry management with multi-entity posting, reversing entries, and drill-down from any balance to its source transactions. Supports intercompany eliminations and automated consolidation entries.

Chart of Accounts

Hierarchical account structure with account grouping, sub-types, and segment coding. Map your QBO or Xero chart to an enterprise-grade structure without changing your existing ledger.

Fiscal Periods

Define fiscal years, periods, and adjustment periods. Lock closed periods to prevent unauthorized postings. Supports calendar and custom period structures.

Recurring Entries

Automate repeating journal entries on daily, weekly, monthly, or custom schedules. Set start and end dates, auto-reverse options, and approval requirements for each template.

Month-End Close

Structured close checklist with task assignment, due dates, dependencies, and sign-off tracking. Track close cycle time and identify bottlenecks across entities and periods.

Multi-Currency

Transact and report in any currency. Automatic exchange rate updates, unrealized gain/loss calculations, and currency revaluation at period end. Full ASC 830 translation support.

Revenue Recognition

ASC 606 & Contract Revenue

Automated revenue recognition that handles multi-element arrangements, milestone billing, percentage-of-completion, and contract modifications under ASC 606.

RevRec Dashboard

Real-time overview of recognized vs. deferred revenue across all contracts. Spot trends, identify at-risk contracts, and drill into any balance for full transaction-level detail.

Contract Management

Create and track revenue contracts with performance obligations, standalone selling prices, and variable consideration. Supports multi-element arrangements and bundled offerings.

Percentage of Completion

Track project completion using cost-to-cost, units-of-delivery, or milestone methods. Automatic revenue calculation based on measured progress and estimate-at-completion updates.

Milestones & Modifications

Define milestone-based recognition triggers tied to deliverables. Handle contract modifications with prospective or cumulative catch-up treatment as required by ASC 606.

Revenue Waterfall

Visualize revenue recognition schedules across time with waterfall charts. See how current bookings translate to future recognized revenue by period, contract, or customer.

Recognition Schedules & Reports

Generate detailed recognition schedules for each contract and performance obligation. Produce disclosure-ready reports for ASC 606 footnotes and audit workpapers.

Sales & Receivables

Invoicing, Collections & Customer Management

End-to-end accounts receivable with smart dunning, aging analysis, and a client self-service portal.

Invoices

Create, send, and track invoices with multi-currency support, line-item detail, project allocation, and approval workflows. Integrates with QBO/Xero billing for seamless sync.

Customers

Centralized customer master with contact details, payment terms, credit limits, and transaction history. Link customers across entities for consolidated AR visibility.

Aging Reports

Current, 30, 60, 90, and 120+ day aging buckets by customer, entity, or project. Export to Excel, schedule for auto-delivery, and drill into any aging bucket for transaction detail.

Smart Dunning

Automated collection reminders with configurable escalation rules, email templates, and follow-up schedules. Pause dunning for disputed invoices and track collector effectiveness.

Client Portal

Self-service portal where customers view open invoices, download statements, make payments, and submit disputes. Reduces AR team workload and accelerates collections.

Procurement, POs & Vendor Management

Full procure-to-pay workflow with purchase orders, receiving, 3-way matching, and approval routing.

Vendor Management

Centralized vendor master data with payment terms, 1099 tracking, W-9 management, insurance certificates, and spend history. Vendor performance metrics and compliance status at a glance.

Purchase Orders

Create POs with multi-level approval workflows, budget validation, and project/cost-center allocation. Track PO status from requisition through receipt and invoice matching.

Receiving & 3-Way Match

Record goods received against POs and automatically match PO, receipt, and vendor invoice for 3-way matching. Flag discrepancies for review before payment authorization.

Recurring Bills & Approvals

Automate recurring vendor bills for rent, subscriptions, and retainers. Route all bills through configurable approval workflows with segregation-of-duties enforcement.

Banking

Bank Reconciliation & Feeds

Automated bank feeds, intelligent matching, and multi-account reconciliation across entities.

Bank Reconciliation

Bank Feeds

Automated transaction feeds from connected bank accounts. AI-assisted categorization learns from your patterns to suggest GL coding, project allocation, and entity assignment.

Payroll & Time

Timesheets, Labor Distribution & DCAA Compliance

Enterprise timesheet management with project-level tracking, labor cost distribution, approval workflows, and DCAA-compliant time capture for government contractors.

Timesheets

Daily or weekly timesheet entry with project, task, and labor category allocation. Supports clock-in/out, overtime rules, and configurable pay periods. Enforces 100% labor distribution.

DCAA Compliance

Timekeeping that meets DCAA requirements: daily entry, total-time accounting, supervisor approvals, after-the-fact recording, and floor check support. Audit-ready from day one.

Labor Distribution

Distribute labor costs across projects, cost pools, and indirect categories. Calculate loaded labor rates with fringe, overhead, and G&A allocations for accurate project costing.

Holidays & Leave

Configure company holiday calendars, PTO accrual policies, and leave types. Employees request time off through the self-service portal with manager approval routing.

Payroll Processing

Payroll processing with multi-state support, tax withholding calculations, and direct deposit. Integrates with popular payroll providers or runs standalone for full control.

GL & Aging Reports
