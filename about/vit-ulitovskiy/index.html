About Vit

Vit Ulitovskiy is a seasoned finance leader with over two decades of experience transforming how organizations manage financial planning, analysis, and reporting. With an MBA and deep functional expertise in FP&A, corporate strategy, and M&A integrations, Vit brings a rare combination of strategic vision and hands-on execution to every engagement.

Throughout his career, Vit has held senior finance roles across healthcare, engineering, and consulting industries - managing teams, building financial models, and leading multi-entity consolidations. His experience spans everything from early-stage growth companies to large enterprises navigating complex mergers and system integrations.

At Ledger Summit

As Finance Leader at Ledger Summit, Vit focuses on designing and implementing AI-powered automation that eliminates repetitive financial workflows. His philosophy is simple: finance teams should spend their time on analysis and strategy, not data entry and reconciliation.

Vit has led the development of automation solutions for bank reconciliation, cash flow forecasting, and project EAC management - helping clients save thousands of hours annually while improving accuracy and audit-readiness.
