Privacy Policy

Read how Ledger Summit handles account information, connected financial data, and privacy protections.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Ledger Summit, we may collect:

  • Account information (name, email address, company name)
  • Financial data accessed through integrations you authorize (e.g., QuickBooks Online, bank feeds)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used, timestamps)
  • Device and browser information for security and compatibility

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our bookkeeping automation, accounting, and FP&A services
  • Process and reconcile financial data on your behalf
  • Communicate with you about your account and service updates
  • Ensure the security and integrity of the platform

3. Third-Party Integrations

Ledger Summit connects to third-party services such as QuickBooks Online, Xero, and banking platforms to provide its features. When you authorize an integration, we access only the data necessary to deliver the Services. We do not sell your financial data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and access controls. We use cloud infrastructure providers that maintain SOC 2 compliance. We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the Services or as required by law.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating the platform (hosting, analytics)
  • Third-party integrations you explicitly authorize
  • Law enforcement when required by applicable law

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. You may also disconnect third-party integrations through your account settings.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain session state and security. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

The Services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@ledgersummit.com.
