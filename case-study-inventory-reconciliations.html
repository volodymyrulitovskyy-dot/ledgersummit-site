Inventory Reconciliation Automation Case Study

A product-based business replaced manual inventory tie-outs with an automated reconciliation process and exception controls.

Client profile: Consumer products business, USD 24M revenue, 7-person accounting and ops finance team.

61% fasterInventory rec cycle
48% lowerUnexplained variance value
73% fewerManual adjustment entries

Before automation

Inventory reconciliation was manual and reactive, with major variances found late in close and limited traceability to root cause.

  • Subledger and GL compared manually in spreadsheets
  • Variance analysis delayed until late close days
  • Adjustments posted with uneven documentation

What Ledger Summit implemented

Ledger Summit deployed automated inventory rec checks with threshold logic, exception queues, and standardized adjustment support.

  • Recurring subledger-to-GL tie-out workflow
  • Variance thresholds by product group and location
  • Standardized issue-to-adjustment resolution flow

Implementation timeline

  • Week 1: baseline variance profile and workflow map
  • Week 2: threshold rule and queue configuration
  • Week 3: pilot run for two major inventory groups
  • Week 4: expansion to all inventory accounts
