Payroll Reconciliations Automation Case Study

A mid-size operator automated payroll reconciliation workflows to reduce tie-out delays and improve period-end accuracy.

Client profile: Business services company, USD 16M revenue, 5-person accounting team.

67% fasterPayroll rec completion time
62% fewerUnresolved rec exceptions
2.3xFaster mismatch resolution

Before automation

Payroll recs depended on manual file comparisons and ad hoc notes, leading to delayed corrections and period-close stress.

  • Multiple exports manually compared each period
  • Mismatch ownership unclear across teams
  • Corrections often pushed into next period

What Ledger Summit implemented

Ledger Summit built an exception-first payroll rec workflow with automated comparisons, owner routing, and SLA escalation.

  • Automated payroll-to-GL comparison routines
  • Mismatch queue by account and cost center owner
  • SLA-based reminders and escalation trail

Implementation timeline

  • Week 1: source mapping and tolerance definition
  • Week 2: comparison workflow and queue setup
  • Week 3: controlled pilot with two payroll cycles
  • Week 4: go-live and KPI baseline reset
